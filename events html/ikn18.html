IIT KGP NUKKAD:2K18

Performing in one of the most prestigious cultural extravaganza of the nation was a next level challenge soaking up all the determination. It was even a greater challenge when it was performed by 18 noobs under the leadership of 3 pre-finals and 1 sophomore. Pantomime performed a Nukkad named “” which portrayed - Truth is never absolute. It is a many-faced manifestation of thoughts. Different people share different stories and the society being judgemental sides with one voice without understanding the depth of the situation. It showed fictional and factual stories regarding a rape claimed by the “victim”. The girl voices her story whereas the boy couldn’t since he was already treated as the rapist. Before committing suicide, he told his part of the story knowing he won’t get justice. Though we weren’t able to win, the oppressive façade of the society was very well depicted through the act.
